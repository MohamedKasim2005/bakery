DON BOSCO TECH SOCIETY



Don Bosco, also known as Saint John Bosco, dedicated his life to the education and 
well-being of impoverished and neglected youth, particularly street children. He 
developed a unique educational approach, the Salesian Preventive System, based on 
reason, religion, and loving-kindness, emphasizing building positive relationships 
with students. Don Bosco's work extended to creating schools, orphanages, and youth 
centers, ultimately founding the Salesian religious order to continue his mission.
